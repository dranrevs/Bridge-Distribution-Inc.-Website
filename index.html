Our Story

About Bridge
Distribution

For over 25 years, Bridge Distribution, Inc. has been the trusted link between world-class technology brands and the Filipino market — built on integrity, powered by expertise.

Connecting Technology
to Every Filipino

Bridge Distribution, Inc. was founded with a singular vision: to be the most reliable and professional technology distributor in the Philippines. From our earliest days as a regional IT distributor in Metro Manila, we have steadily grown our operations, our people, and our brand partnerships to serve the entire country.

Our portfolio today spans the most respected names in the IT industry — from computing and enterprise infrastructure to creative peripherals, displays, networking, audio equipment, and printing solutions.

"We don't just distribute products. We build the bridges that connect world-class technology to every Filipino — in every city, every store, every home."

Today, Bridge Distribution is recognized by our principal brands and channel partners alike as a distributor that delivers on its commitments — every order, every time.
